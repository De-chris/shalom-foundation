About Shalom's Foundation

Shalom Foundations is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering teens, students, and youths through skill acquisition, education, and job creation.

  • Provide skills training in various areas to enhance employability
  • Create job opportunities for young people
  • Foster responsibility and leadership among youths
  • Support youth development to help them reach their full potential
